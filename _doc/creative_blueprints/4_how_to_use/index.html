Core Capabilities

Day One

Everything the solo founder needs. Nothing they don't.

Three categories, one priority view — designed to make the right next call obvious every single day.

📋

Lead Logger

Add a new contact in seconds — name, business type (interior designer, restaurant, or boutique), and a first-impression note. Every lead starts here.

🌡️

Interest Tracker

Mark each lead's warmth — cold, warm, or hot — and update it after every touchpoint. The app uses this to rank who deserves your next call.

✅

Follow-Up Queue

A ranked list of who to contact next, surfaced automatically. Open the app and your #1 priority is already waiting — no scanning, no guessing.

🗂️

Category Filter

Switch instantly between interior designers, restaurants, and fashion boutiques. Each vertical behaves differently — keep them separate, work them separately.
