# The Curated Co — Lead Tracking App

## Product Overview
A lean, focused lead tracking tool built for a solo founder doing direct outreach. The app helps track prospective clients across three verticals: interior designers, restaurants, and fashion boutiques.

## Users
- Single user: the solo founder
- No team collaboration needed
- Mobile-friendly preferred (outreach happens on the go)

## Core Purpose
- Track leads and their current status in the outreach pipeline
- Capture interest level for each lead (e.g., Hot / Warm / Cold)
- Surface who to follow up with next based on interest and last contact date
- Log notes and interactions per lead

## Lead Verticals
- Interior Designers
- Restaurants
- Fashion Boutiques

## Strategic Principles
- Simplicity over features — no bloat
- Clarity first — the founder should immediately know what to do next
- Fast to update — logging a touchpoint should take seconds
- Visual priority cues — highlight overdue follow-ups and high-interest leads

## Brand & Tone
- Clean, elevated, minimal
- Feels like a well-designed notebook, not enterprise software
- Name: The Curated Co
- Tone: confident, purposeful, refined
